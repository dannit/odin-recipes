Overnight Oats

Description

Overnight oats are a great way to eat a healthy protein packed breakfast that tastes amazing. They are quick and easy to prepare The night before and will save you time in the morning When you need to get out of the house in a rush. We will be using simple household ingredients That you should already have. Theese are great, because with simple variations you can change to different flavors that will keep breakfast exciting.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Rolled Oats
  • 3/4 cups Whole Milk
  • 3 Tablespoons Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Tablespoon Maple Syrup
  • 2 Tablespoons Collagen Peptides
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Bannana
  • 3 Strawberries

Steps

  1. Add oats to 32 ounce glass jar
  2. Add milk, yogurt, maple syrup, peptides, and vanilla. Then close the jar and shake and till combined
  3. Chop up, strawberries, and bananas, and add to container
  4. Leave in fridge overnight (at least 3 hours), enjoy your speedy breakfast in the morning!
