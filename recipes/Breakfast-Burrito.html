Breakfast Burrito

Description

Make this tasty breakfast burrito to elavate your breakfast expirence. With crispy sausage, creamy eggs, and melty cheese this burrito is truely like no other. Not to mention its pack with protien and vital fats that your body needs to thrive.

Ingreadients

  • 1 Flour Tortilla
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/4 lb Raw Uncased Sausage
  • 1/8 cup Pepperjack Cheese
  • 1/2 Avocado

Steps

  1. Scramble eggs with cheese until they start to thicken. Make sure to take them off the heat early as they will cook longer in the pan
  2. Form sausage into a patty and cook until crispy and golden brown
  3. Warm up tortilla in a large pan on low heat
  4. Remove tortilla from pan and add ingreadents into the center
  5. Roll into burrito and enjoy!
